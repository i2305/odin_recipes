Spiked Lemonade

Homemade lemonade spiked with rum by allrecipes magazin. Recipe by yoly.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1/4 white sugar
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 3 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers coconut-flavored rum
  • ice cubes, as needed

Steps

  1. Place sugar in warm water, stirring until sugar is completely dissolved. Add lemon juice, cold water and coconut rum. Stir until completely incorporated.
  2. Fill two glasses with ice cubes and divide lemon juice mixture amongst both glasses.
