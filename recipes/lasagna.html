Lasagna

World's best lasagna by allrecipes magazin. Recipe by John Chandler.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves chrused garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, divided ot to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black peper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, onion, ground beef and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat, until well browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 tablespoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds and peper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley and 1/2 tablespoon salt.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  4. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in a bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure that foil does not touch the cheese.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
